Warehouse Scanner Help
How to Use:
  1. Click "Start Camera" to begin scanning
  2. Point camera at barcode to scan
  3. Follow workflow instructions
  4. Use "Test Barcodes" page for testing
Workflows:
  • Picking: Scan ord_ barcode, then scan items
  • Stock Count: Scan stc_ barcode, then count items
  • Location Move: Scan loc_ barcode, follow steps
  • Returns: Scan item barcode when no workflow active
